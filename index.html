What You Can Do

  • Zero-shot SNP & region scoring
  • Zero-shot structural variant (SV) scoring
  • XGBoost classifiers (TIS/TTS/splice sites)
  • In-silico mutagenesis
  • Fine-tuned PlantCAD2 (LoRA) for chromatin / expression / translation
  • Pre-training / fine-tuning from scratch
  • Model recommendations (which model, inference speed, GPU memory)

Related Tools

  • GeneCAD: an in-silico genome annotation pipeline for plants built on top of PlantCAD2. View on GitHub.

Science / Highlights

PlantCAD1: pretrained on 16 angiosperm genomes (~160M years of evolution), 512bp single-nucleotide resolution, masked language modeling; SOTA cross-species performance on TIS/TTS/splice donor & acceptor; zero-shot identifies deleterious mutations and known causal variants (e.g. sweet corn Su1 W578R).

PlantCAD2: long-context (8192bp) successor for cross-species functional annotation in angiosperms; three model sizes; supports zero-shot SNP/SV scoring and LoRA fine-tuning for downstream tasks.
